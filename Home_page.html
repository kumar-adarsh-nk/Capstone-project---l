 »  Portal Introduction and Important points to be noted:


  • A Ration Grievance Portal is an online platform designed to help citizens report issues related to the Public Distribution System (PDS).

  • It provides a transparent and user-friendly system for lodging complaints regarding ration cards, food quality, quantity, or delivery delays.

  • The portal allows users to submit grievances anytime and from anywhere, reducing the need to visit government offices.

  • The portal allows users to submit grievances anytime and from anywhere, reducing the need to visit government offices.

  • The system helps improve efficiency, transparency, and fairness in ration distribution through giving the following access:

      »  Access of an admin (government officer)

      1. An admin is a gov. officer who is appointed by the government of India.
      2. Having the full access of the grievances portal.
      3. The admin creates and manages accounts for distributors and beneficiaries.
      4. Admin oversees all activities related to ration distribution and system operations.

      »  Access of Local/Area distributer.

      1. Distributor verifies beneficiary identity and documents through KYC.
      2. Distributor provides ration items such as rice, wheat, and sugar to beneficiaries.
      3. Record the quantity of ration distributed to each beneficiary.
      4. Inform the admin about stock shortages or system issues.

      »  Access of a beneficiary.

      1. Beneficiaries create an account using their ration card and personal details.
      2. Beneficiaries can see which distributor or ration shop serves their area.
      3. Beneficiaries can view their ration distribution status and eligibility.
      4. Beneficiaries can report problems related to ration distribution.

  • It empowers citizens by giving them a direct voice to communicate problems and receive updates on complaint status.

  • Overall, the portal strengthens trust between the government and beneficiaries by promoting good governance and service quality.
